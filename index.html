Monthly Fee Clarification

Some services require a monthly fee because they use artificial intelligence tools, hosting, or external integrations (like Make, n8n, OpenAI). They also include technical support and minor adjustments.

The website service works without monthly fees if the client manages their content. If you prefer us to do it, it can be activated for $15/month.

Why do some services have a monthly fee?

For some services like catalogs, QR orders or AI chatbots, we apply a small monthly fee. This covers real costs such as:

Hosting and system maintenance

Keeping your service running 24/7 with optimal performance

AI processing for images and texts

Automatic image enhancement and intelligent text generation

External integrations

Use of Make, n8n or AI APIs (like GPT) for automation

Updates and technical support

Basic technical support and minor adjustments

No monthly fee is charged if the service doesn't require it (like the Web One Page), but you can request an optional maintenance plan if you want the Tiva team to manage constant changes or improvements for you.

This ensures your system always works without problems and you can focus on growing your business.
